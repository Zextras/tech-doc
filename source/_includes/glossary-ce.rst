
.. glossary::
   :sorted:

   Member
     A **Member** is a user that takes part to a chat or a Group.

   Participant
     A **Participant** is a user that takes part to a Meeting.

   Group
     A **Group** is a chat between three or more users.

   Meeting
     A **Meeting** is a video call between two or more users.

   Moderator  
     The *Moderator* of a Group is a special user, which by default is
     the creator of the group. Moderators can add users to the group,
     promote other members and carry out some administrative tasks on
     the chat.
